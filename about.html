Our Story

From a Workshop to a Global Movement.

Stackly AI was founded in 2018 by a group of AI researchers and manufacturing veterans who saw a glaring gap: industrial operations were sitting on mountains of data but lacked the intelligence to use it. We built the first predictive maintenance prototype in a garage workshop outside Berlin — and never looked back.

Within two years, our platform was deployed across 50 factories in Europe. Today, Stackly AI powers operations in 30+ countries, processing over 2 billion sensor data points daily. We've grown from a founding team of 4 to more than 1,200 employees spanning AI research, robotics engineering, and industrial deployment.

Dr. Elena Voss
CEO & Co-Founder, Stackly AI

To make world-class AI and robotics accessible to every industrial operation — regardless of size, location, or industry. We believe intelligent automation should be a tool for empowerment, not exclusivity.

Our Vision

We envision a future where factories, warehouses, and energy plants run themselves — continuously learning, adapting, and optimizing with minimal human intervention. A world where industrial accidents are rare and downtime is a memory.
